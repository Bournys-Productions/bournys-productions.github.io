Information

Overview

UK:RP | SRP is a realism-focused emergency services roleplay experience built around a 1:1 recreation of Shirebrook. The aim is to provide immersive scenes, detailed locations, and a professional roleplay environment for all players.

Roles & Services

Players can take part in a range of emergency service roles, including policing, fire and rescue, ambulance, and specialist response teams. Each service is designed to offer its own responsibilities, vehicles, and style of roleplay.

Roleplay & Progression

The experience is built to reward professional roleplay, teamwork, and progression through activity and involvement. Whether attending incidents, supporting large scenes, or working through specialist pathways, there is always room to develop.

Community & Events

The community is supported through regular updates, organised roleplay sessions, and a growing range of events. This helps keep the experience active, varied, and enjoyable for both new and returning players.

Experience

The goal of SRP is to deliver a polished and engaging UK roleplay experience that feels structured, realistic, and enjoyable. From routine patrols to large-scale incidents, players can expect a detailed environment built for immersive gameplay.
