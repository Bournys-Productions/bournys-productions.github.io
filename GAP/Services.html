Services & Benefits

1. Overview

The Game Assist Program (GAP) provides structured support for developers through development assistance, media creation, and marketing services. Our goal is to help improve game quality, visibility, and long-term growth through consistent support and promotion.

2. Development Support

We provide development guidance to help improve gameplay systems, performance, structure, and overall game quality. This may include feedback on mechanics, optimisation advice, and general improvement suggestions to help your project reach a higher standard.

3. Media Creation

Developers may receive media support including thumbnails, promotional images, branding assistance, and short-form visual content. This helps improve presentation quality and ensures your game is more appealing to new players.

4. Marketing Services

Games in the program may be promoted through Bournys Productions’ official platforms, including our Discord community and social media channels. Marketing support is used to increase visibility, attract players, and build awareness around your project.

5. Social Media Promotion

Selected projects may be featured on Bournys Productions social accounts. This includes posts, highlights, announcements, and updates designed to showcase your game to a wider audience.

6. Program Benefits

Developers who join the Game Assist Program may receive access to free marketing support through Bournys Productions’ platforms. This includes promotional exposure that would otherwise require external advertising resources.

7. Visibility & Growth Support

The program is designed to improve game visibility by combining development support with active promotion. This helps developers reach new players, improve retention, and build stronger long-term engagement.

8. Important Note

All services provided through the Game Assist Program are intended to support development and improve potential performance. No guarantees are made regarding revenue, player count, or game success.
